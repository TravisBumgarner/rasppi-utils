## 8. Bluesky — how discovery actually works

Verified mechanics (official AT Proto docs + live feed inspection):
- **No platform hashtag algorithm.** Feeds are third-party generator services: either keyword/tag matchers or curator **allowlists**. Tags only matter insofar as a specific feed matches them.
- **Load-bearing tags** (feed triggers observed): `#photography`, `#fotografia` (top photo feed matches both — Spanish tag has real reach), `#landscape`, `#landscapephotography`, `#wildlife`, `#nature`, `#naturephotography`, `#astro`, `#nationalparks`, `#streetphotography`, `#filmphotography`, `#believeinfilm` (THE film community tag, ex-Twitter), themed days (#MountainMonday).
- **Curated feeds worth pursuing** (allowlist — you must be selected):
  - *Landscape Photography* by `@bluesky.photography` — active July 2026, "selected photographers," small (375 likes) but exactly your subject. Engage the curator.
  - *Top Nature Photography* by `@nickchillphoto.com` — entry = comment on the feed's pinned post; triggers then #landscape/#wildlife/#seascape/#astro/#nature; **alt text required, no AI**.
- **Config fix:** `"#filmphotography 🎞️"` as a literal tag is malformed — the 🎞️ emoji is a *caption convention* (📷 camera / 🎞️ film stock lines), not a tag. Remove the emoji variants; keep `#filmphotography` + `#believeinfilm`, and put the gear line in the caption body.
- Alt text on every Bluesky photo post — several curated feeds require it.
