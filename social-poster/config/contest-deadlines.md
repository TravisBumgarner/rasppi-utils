# Photo contest deadlines

Feature pipeline beyond hashtags. Maintained by the `/find-contests` skill
(sources + filter rules live there); facts verified against official
rules/terms pages, last sweep **July 8, 2026**. ⚠ = couldn't fully confirm.

Filter rules: must be open to Mexico residents or US residents; no
rights-grab TOS (perpetual/irrevocable/commercial licenses on ALL entries,
"entries become property of", moral-rights waivers); no engagement mills.

## Rejected — rights grabs, mills, or ineligible

| Contest | Why |
|---|---|
| **Lomography** (all site competitions incl. TEN AND ONE qualifiers) | ❌ **Rights:** uploading to lomography.com grants a "royalty-free, perpetual, irrevocable… worldwide licence" to use/modify/derivative-work your photos "for **any editorial or commercial purpose**," uncompensated, on ALL uploads — not just winners. Weekly free contests, perfect film fit, terrible terms. Enter only if OK donating commercial use of the entry. |
| **Tamron Americas User Photo Contest** | ❌ **Rights:** ALL entries — perpetual, worldwide license to "use, reproduce, edit… make derivative works of and/or otherwise exploit in any manner… without compensation." Gear fits (Tamron Di/Di III, EXIF required), Mexico eligible; terms don't. |
| **Ricoh GR PHOTO FESTIVAL** | ❌ **Rights:** ALL entries — irrevocable perpetual license "for advertising… **or for any other reason**," zero compensation, and there are no prizes (exhibition only). |
| **Smithsonian Magazine Photo Contest** | ❌ **Rights:** ALL entries — perpetual license incl. derivative works "in any media… for any purpose," extendable to third parties Smithsonian authorizes. |
| **AlaskaTravel.com contest** | ❌ **Rights:** "all entered photos become property of Alaska Tour & Travel." Also likely US-only ⚠, $500 prize. |
| **CEWE Photo Award** | ❌ Irrevocable perpetual affiliate-wide license incl. third-party photobook distribution, all entries. |
| **IPPAWARDS** | ⚠ Skipped on value: pay per photo ($7.50–$150.50) while granting a promo license on every entry. Terms improved (June 2026: competition-scoped, credited) but pay-to-license-your-own-work is a poor trade. |
| **GuruShots / ViewBug / Photocrowd** | Engagement mills (pay-to-win voting / subscription upsells), not juried contests. Never list. |
| National Park Photo Contest (Tamron/Outside) | Ineligible: US residents (50 states + DC) 21+ only. |
| Sigma Power of F1.2 (and Sigma generally) | Ad-hoc campaigns only, most recent was US-only + specific F1.2 Art lenses; no recurring program exists. |
| Pentax Photo Contest (Japan) / Ricoh GR weekly contest | Japan-only (dormant since 2023 anyway) / US+Canada-only with "any other reason" license. No Pentax film contest exists despite listicle claims. |
| Apple "Shot on iPhone" Challenge | Dormant since 2022. If revived: rights were caution-OK post-2019 (winners get paid licensing fee), so re-check on announcement. |
| Yashica / TLR-specific contests | None exist from any official organizer. |
