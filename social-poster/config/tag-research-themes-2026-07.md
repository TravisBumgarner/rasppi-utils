# Theme tag research — new PhotoTypes — July 2026

Companion to [tag-research-2026-07.md](tag-research-2026-07.md) (hubs/geo audit) and
the gallery analysis in [../analysis/](../analysis/). Covers the 12 candidate
PhotoTypes surfaced by clustering 27k AI-tagged photos. Same rules as before:
hubs verified against the account itself or the brand's own site; hashtag-generator
listicles rejected as sources. ✅ verified · ⚠️ partial · ❓ unverified.

**Big picture:** two kinds of themes emerged.
1. **Themes with real curator hubs** — fog/moody, dogs, food, night, road trips,
   street art, hiking. These get `priority` entries.
2. **Themes with no dedicated curator** — mountains, forest, water, winter, desert.
   Searches surfaced only SEO slop or individual portfolios. These ride the big
   nature mega-hubs (below) + your geo/park hubs, and get strong `general` +
   `bluesky` tags instead.

## The nature mega-hubs (shared by mountains / forest / water / winter)

| Hub | Size | Mechanism | Confidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| `@wilderness_culture` / `#wildernessculture` | 2M | tag in caption — the hashtag is in the account's own bio | ✅ |
| `@discoverearth` / `#discoverearth` | 7M | tag + mention; repost-driven feed | ⚠️ account verified, feature rules not published |
| `@earthpix` / `#earthpix` | ~14M | tag + mention | ⚠️ same |
| `@roamtheplanet` / `#roamtheplanet` | large | tag + mention | ❓ |

Odds per post are low at this scale, but the cost is one tag. Recommend putting
`#wildernessculture` (the only bio-verified one) in `priority` for the outdoor
themes and the others in `general`.

## Notes & caveats

- **Bluesky themed days are real but only two verified:** `#MountainMonday` and
  `#TidesOutTuesday` were observed live in the curated Landscapes feed (July 2026).
  Others likely exist (the convention carried over from Twitter nature communities) —
  spot them in-feed before adding.
- **Mega-hub rights caveat:** big repost hubs (earthpix-class) have a history of
  loose credit practices — they repost with credit but engagement flows to them.
  Cost is just a tag, but don't expect NatGeo-style attribution.
- **Not covered:** a dedicated mountain hub, desert hub, or water hub may exist as
  a mid-size account that web search can't surface (Instagram search is walled).
  Worth 10 minutes of in-app exploration of the suggested tags to spot mid-size
  curators (50-500K) that repost with credit.
